# Manuscript Highlights

- **Solar-Resilient Precursor Detection**: Proposes a novel SE-GNN architecture that integrates Kp and Dst indices to filter solar storm noise during the 2025 Solar Maximum.
- **Adaptive Station Weighting**: Introduces Squeeze-and-Excitation (SE) blocks to dynamically weight geolocation sensors based on signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) in the ULF band.
- **Operational Redundancy**: Quantifies system resilience showing >60% accuracy maintenance during multi-station dropout (N-1, N-2) scenarios.
- **Real-Time Efficiency**: Achieves a benchmark inference latency of 0.23 seconds on standard CPU hardware, enabling deployment in automated early-warning pipelines.
- **Explainable Geophysics**: Validates model focus on lithospheric ULF ($0.01 - 0.1$ Hz) signatures via comparative Grad-CAM frequency analysis.
